About




Coding club is a social academic group focused on help university students improve their coding skills and capabilities.
Passionate Indivituals get together to learn, build and be innovative. The Coding Club allows its members to work on
collaborative projects ranging from web development to game design, often using languages such as:
PYTHON
JavaScript
C++

Coding Club Objectives


The Club fosters critical thinking skill development through interactive workshops, hackathons, and coding challenges while
encouraging teamwork and problem-solving. Beginners recieve guidence from experienced programmers, who willing share their vast
knowledge,creating an environment where everyone can grow. Additionally, more often than not, contribute to open-source
projects, participate in competitions and explore emerging technologies. Thid allows the club to become a haven for creativity,
networking and career development in the tech industry.

What are you waiting for? Join us NOW!!!

Membership


Membership in the coding club is open to all who are interested in programming, irrespective of skill level. Be it a beginner
looking to learn the basics or an experienced developer eager to collaborate on advanced projects, there is a spot for everyone.
Members are encouraged to actively participate in workshops, coding challenges, and team projects to enhance their skills and
contribute to the academic community. Regular attendance at meetings and events helps foster a collaborative environment where
ideas and knowledge are shared.
