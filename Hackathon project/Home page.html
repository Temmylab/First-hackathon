What is the Coding Club



The Eduvos coding club is a social academic group focused teachering and aiding those interested in IT
or software design. They operate out of and around the midrand campus of eduvos, the club offers:

  • Workshops
  • Coding lessons
  • Event meet ups
  • Software experience for work after university
