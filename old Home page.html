Coding club is a social academic group focused on help university students improve
their coding skills and capability.
Our mission is to empower students to enhance their coding skills, collaborate on projects, and build strong portfolios. We aim to cultivate a community of innovative and passionate coders.
We offer Workshops, Project Portfolio Building, Collaborative Projects and Networking Events.
Join the Coding Club and turn your ideas into reality . “Code. Create. Collaborate.”
